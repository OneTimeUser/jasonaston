These were a series of tests that delve into the power of surreptitious fusion of fiction and the real with the goal of truly immersive experiences, especially as they related to gameplay.


A focal point of this first test was the use of the browser and scripting techniques to replace existing news or content with relevant game or story related fictional content. The aim was to blur and blend fiction into one's own web navigating reality.


This experiment will lead into further explorations of clever techniques of subterfuge and sleight of hand with the goal being efficacy of immersion.
