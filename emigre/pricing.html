Frequently Asked Questions

Are the prices above the actual prices I will be paying?

The price listed above is the base amount according to each type of evaluation. We quote each applicant's needs accordingly, thus you may be quoted a price greater than what is advertised above.

What if I do not agree with the quoted price?

We are explicit in our price quotes for our services, but also understand that you may still have questions. If you have any problems, we are always available to you. Just reach out.

Can I add more recipients once I've paid for services?

While we assess each case on an individual basis, we as that your information be complete upon submission and receipt of payment of services. This is to ensure that we give you accurate and appropriate information in a timely manner.

I do not see a price for Professional Licensing evaluation?

In this case, you would choose the bespoke service initially, then we would get back to you with costs accordingly.

Can I try before I buy?

Because our services are advisory, we cannot offer this service just yet.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

On a case by case basis, should a group of persons, e.g. a family, need advisory services, we do offer discounts on multiple applications.

Is this a subscription service?

This is not a subscription service.
