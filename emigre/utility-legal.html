Website Visitors

From time to time, Émigré may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Émigré also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on https://emigre.app blog posts. Émigré only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below.

Certain visitors to Émigré's websites choose to interact with Émigré in ways that require Émigré to gather personally-identifying information. The amount and type of information that Émigré gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who sign up for a blog at https://emigre.app to provide a username and email address.

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Protection of Certain Personally-Identifying Information

Émigré discloses potentially personally-identifying and personally-identifying information only to those of its employees, contractors and affiliated organizations that (i) need to know that information in order to process it on Émigré's behalf or to provide services available at Émigré's website, and (ii) that have agreed not to disclose it to others. Aggregated Statistics

Émigré may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its website. Émigré may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Émigré does not disclose your personally-identifying information.

Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, Émigré uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Émigré uses cookies to help Émigré identify and track visitors, their usage of https://emigre.app, and their website access preferences. Émigré visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Émigré's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Émigré's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Émigré's use of cookies.
