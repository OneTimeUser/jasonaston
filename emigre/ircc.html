Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada - IRCC

Need an Evaluation Credential Assesment, ECA, for a Canadian application for immigration, citizenship or study?

You will need to have your education assessed if you are planning to immigrate to Canada as part of the Federal Skilled Worker program or in a specific profession or tade, or if you are a prospective student.

An ECA is used to verify that your foreign degree, diploma, or certificate (or other proof of your credential) is valid and equal to a Canadian one.

Emigré can help to determine exactly which credentials you should be submitting for your ECA, saving you time and headaches, while also maximizing your 'points' in the Comprehensive Ranking System.

Emigré will help you frame your education so that you can earn the most points credited towards your application. We also are experienced in advising you on what your degree equivalency would be. Knowing this information ahead of time will reduce any surprise errors or holdups in the process.

For more information on the Evaluation Credential Assessment for Canada, please view this link to Canada's own page on the ECA.

For the Federal Skilled Worker Program - FSWP

If you are applying for the Federal Skilled Worker Program, you must have your credentials assessed and provide an ECA as part of your application.

This usually includes completed foreign education as documented by the following:

  • diploma
  • certificate
  • degree
  • other proof

For certain professional jobs or trades

If you are applying to enter Canada because of a professional, trade or other regulated job, you may need an ECA to accompany your application. This are can get notoriously complicated, so it's best you get in touch so we can help point you in the right direction.

For non-regulated jobs

An ECA is encouraged for immigrants even for non-regulated/professional jobs in that this document still helps employers understand your qualifications and education.

For studying

An ECA is necessary when applying to study in Canada.
